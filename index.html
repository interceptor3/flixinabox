Flix in a Box

What is it?

Knowledge and gear for producing high quality video stories. Watch my TED Talk which explains it:

The Premise

You are walking around with high definition video production equipment right in your pocket: your smartphone. With Flix in a Box you can use it to make really effective videos for your business, your non-profit, your news, your blog, anything for which you need to tell a story!

The Problem

Making high quality videos used to be expensive, time-consuming and difficult and telling a good story with video even more so.

The Solution

Flix in a Box brings you the know-how and the equipment so you can tell your story using video.

The Components

Camera or smartphone, tripod, microphone, LED light, stand and a sturdy case to put it all in... plus instructions that tell you how to use it all.

Putting it all together

You have choices for putting it all together: edit on your own smartphone (if it's a simple edit), edit on your laptop using iMovie or any other (free) video editing software or hire an editor to do it for you
