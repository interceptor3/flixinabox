What is it?

A kit with instructions for producing high quality videos. Watch my TED Talk which explains it:

The Premise

You are walking around with high definition video production equipment right in your pocket: your smartphone. With Flix in a Box you can use it to make really effective videos for your business

The Problem

Making high quality videos used to be expensive, time-consuming and difficult and telling a good story with video even more so

The Solution

Flix in a Box bring you the equipment and the know-how to make great videos

The Components

Camera, tripod, microphone, LED light, stand and a sturdy case to put it all in... plus instructions that tell you how to use it all.
